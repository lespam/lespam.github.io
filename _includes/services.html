Software Testing

Definition: Software testing is the process of evaluating a system to check if it satisfies its business requirements, measuring its quality in terms of attributes such as correctness, completeness, usability, and performance.

Why Testing is Required: Testing is essential to provide assurance to stakeholders that the software works as intended, avoid reputation damage due to defects, reduce costs by detecting issues early, save development time, and bring a different perspective to product development.

I offer seasonal, contract, consultation, and freelance services as a QA analyst. I specialize in utilizing various static and dynamic test design techniques. My expertise encompasses the following key areas:

  • Test Planning and execution
    I create comprehensive test plans and test scenarios derived from use cases, helping ensure end-to-end testing.
  • Defect Management
    I identify and report bugs and defects detected during testing, including a thorough description of the issue.
  • Documentation and Reporting
    I maintain test documentation, including test plans, test cases,test reports and provide test metrics.
  • User Acceptance Testing (UAT)
    I execute User Acceptance Testing to ensure the compliance of end-users' requirements.
  • Ad-hoc Testing
    I perform ad-hoc testing to identify issues quickly in less structured scenarios.
