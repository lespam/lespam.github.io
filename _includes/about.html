So, I didn't give up. I decided to learn everything I could about humans, psychology, sofware and design principles. 🧠 I decided to change my perspective and see problems as opportunities to solve. I decided to ensure actions that enable systems where people can communicate securely and efficiently. 🔐

My Projects 📚

One of the projects that I'm most proud of is Cara a Cara, an installation that uses facial recognition technology to create an exercise of memory and awareness about the disappearance in Mexico. It identifies similarities between the face of the person who experiences the installation and the faces of thousands of people who have disappeared in Mexico. 😢 We wanted to preserve and promote the memory and dignity of these people, as well as raise awareness and empathy among the public. 💖

Check out Cara a Cara project.

Another project that I really enjoyed working on was Justicia Transparente, a tool that analyzes and visualizes the sentences issued by the Supreme Court of Justice of Mexico. 🇲🇽 It uses natural language processing and machine learning techniques to extract relevant information from the sentences, such as the topics, actors, arguments, and votes involved. 🧠 It also allows users to explore and compare the sentences according to different criteria, such as date, chamber, minister, or type of case. 📊 We wanted to improve the transparency and accountability of the judicial system in Mexico, as well as foster civic engagement and education. 👏

Check out Justicia Transparente project.
